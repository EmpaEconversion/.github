We address industry-relevant challenges in mobility and stationary energy storage, drawing on expertise in **batteries**, **electrocatalysis**, and **laboratory automation**.

Our laboratory is based at **Empa**, the Swiss Federal Laboratories for Materials Science and Technology, and is affiliated with **ETH Zurich** (Department of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering) and **EPFL** (School of Engineering, Institute of Materials).

This GitHub organization hosts a range of software developed for automating experiments and analyzing experimental data in battery and electrocatalysis research.
